About College

Established in 1995 by the Shadan Educational Society, Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) stands as a pioneering institution affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University. Over its 25-year journey, SCET has grown to offer eight undergraduate and seven postgraduate programs, producing over 10,000 successful engineers. Currently led by Mr. Mohd Shah Alam Rasool Khan, Chairman of Shadan Educational Society, the college emphasizes quality education and innovation. SCET boasts state-of-the-art facilities, a commitment to research and development, and a strong faculty foundation. The institution has consistently produced high-achieving graduates who contribute to various industries globally. With ISO certification and memberships in prestigious organizations, SCET continues to uphold its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

About Us

SCET Coding Club, the pioneering organization at SCET College, was born out of a shared passion for technology and innovation With a vision to provide students with a platform to explore and advance their coding skills, we embarked on a journey that began with a simple idea. As the first-ever club at SCET, our story is one of determination, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

From our humble beginnings, we've evolved into a vibrant community of coders, tech enthusiasts, and problem solvers. Our mission is to nurture the next generation of tech leaders by offering workshops, hackathons, and collaborative coding projects. As we continue to grow, we remain committed to fostering an inclusive environment where creativity knows no bounds.

Join us in shaping the future of technology and being a part of the pioneering spirit that defines SCET Coding Club. Our story is just beginning, and we invite you to be a part of the journey towards a brighter, more innovative future at SCET College.

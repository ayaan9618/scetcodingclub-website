About College

Established in 1995 by the Shadan Educational Society, Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) stands as a pioneering institution affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University. Over its 25-year journey, SCET has grown to offer eight undergraduate and seven postgraduate programs, producing over 10,000 successful engineers. Currently led by Mr. Mohd Shah Alam Rasool Khan, Chairman of Shadan Educational Society, the college emphasizes quality education and innovation. SCET boasts state-of-the-art facilities, a commitment to research and development, and a strong faculty foundation. The institution has consistently produced high-achieving graduates who contribute to various industries globally. With ISO certification and memberships in prestigious organizations, SCET continues to uphold its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

About Club

The SCET Coding club is a student organization that focuses on promoting programming and computer science education in SCET.

The Coding Club at Shadan College of Engineering and Technology (SCET) is a vibrant and student-driven initiative, showcasing the passion and enthusiasm of the student community for coding and programming. Established by proactive students who share a common interest in coding, this club serves as a hub for fostering technical skills, collaboration, and innovation. The Coding Club provides a platform for students to engage in coding competitions, hackathons, and collaborative coding projects. Members actively participate in coding contests, representing SCET and showcasing their skills at various levels. The club organizes regular coding sessions, workshops, and seminars, where students can learn and discuss the latest trends and technologies in the coding world. In addition to honing technical skills, the Coding Club encourages a culture of creativity and problem-solving. Members collaborate on projects that range from app development to solving real-world challenges through coding solutions. The club also promotes teamwork and knowledge-sharing, creating an environment where students can learn from each other and collectively grow as programmers. The Coding Club plays a vital role in enhancing the overall technical acumen of SCET students and prepares them for challenges in the rapidly evolving tech industry. Through its activities and events, the club not only contributes to the academic and technical development of its members but also adds to the dynamic and innovative spirit of SCET as a whole.
